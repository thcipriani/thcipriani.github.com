About Me

Webdevlopius Hackicus

Digital rights ideologue, Vim lover, beer geek, emoji junkie, dotfile tinkerer and unabashed Sriracha addict— are all true (and potentially derisive) things that could be said about me. I am currently a full-stack developer at SparkFun Electronics in sunny Boulder, Colorado.

When I’m not thinking about how to make the web a better, more dynamic place—you’ll find me homebrewing beer, roasting coffee or making the final adjustment to my admittedly already ridiculous collection of dotfiles.
